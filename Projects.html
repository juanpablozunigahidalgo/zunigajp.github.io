Projects

  • Flat Renovation

Due to life circumstances. I had to change my address in Gothenburg. Finding a place to live in this city was quite a challenge. However, I eventually managed to purchase an old flat at a relatively good price, which presented me with an opportunity to renovate it. And that's exactly what I did.

Over the course of 8 months. I worked tirelessly. Even taking on double shifts in a 24/7 mode. The results of my hard work are now evident, and I couldn't be happier with the outcome. The entire project and journey were incredibly fulfilling, as for leaping into a pool from a height of 5 meters. Despite my initial nervousness, I ended up feeling immensely proud of the results, along with the strong mindset I maintained throughout.

In summary, the key lessons I learned are as follows: (1) Seek advice from experienced individuals, (2) Make careful plans before starting something, and (3) Stay mentally strong because unexpected things will happen.

  • Tourism cycling.

It is a passion of mine to cycle long distance routes. For days, weeks, or perhaps months. While crossing countries in the farm side. Enjoying nature. Appreciating the simple things. Discovering cities, culture and architecture. Thankfully. I am not the only one. Many people like that as well.

By combining this passion. With the programming journey, I started some years ago. I created the open source project Tourism cycling. Which is a responsive website. That aids long route cyclists worldwide. With digital tools for route calculation, equipment planning, and essential information. While also providing convenient e-commerce links for finding necessary equipment.

Today. The latest version of this web-app. Is written in react typescript with Mui libraries. With partial user profile information. This enables scalability to build the next steps. Where the goal is to stream personalized equipment recommendations, and route calculations and create a knowledge network for long route cyclists.
